POLICY OVERVIEW
The annual performance bonus program is a strategic component of our total rewards framework, designed to recognize and reward employees for exceptional contributions to individual, team, and company success. This discretionary bonus system aligns employee efforts with organizational objectives while maintaining competitive market positioning.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA (DETAILED)
Service Requirements:
Regular Full-Time Employees: Eligible upon completion of 6 consecutive months of active service by December 31st of the performance year

Part-Time Employees: Pro-rated eligibility based on standard hours worked:

20-29 hours/week: 50% of full-time bonus

30-39 hours/week: 75% of full-time bonus

40+ hours/week: 100% eligibility

New Hires: Employees hired after July 1st receive pro-rated bonus for partial year

Leave Status: Employees on approved leave maintain eligibility if they return by payout date

Exclusion Conditions:
Performance Improvement Plans: Employees on formal PIPs at any point during Q4 are ineligible

Termination Scenarios:

Voluntary resignation before March 15th payout: Full forfeiture

Involuntary termination: Prorated based on active service

Retirement/Disability: Full payout based on service period

Contractors/Consultants: Not eligible under this program

Probationary Period: Must complete probation successfully

BONUS CALCULATION MATRIX
Multi-Tier Calculation Framework:
Final Bonus = Base Bonus × (Individual Multiplier × 60%) × (Company Multiplier × 40%)

Individual Performance Assessment:
Outstanding (Top 10%): 1.5× multiplier

Consistently exceeds all major objectives

Demonstrates exceptional initiative

Significant contributions beyond role scope

Strong (Next 30%): 1.2× multiplier

Meets or exceeds all key objectives

Solid performance with clear business impact

Demonstrates company values consistently

Developing (Middle 50%): 0.8× multiplier

Meets most objectives with some development areas

Performance generally satisfactory

Requires specific improvement areas

Unsatisfactory (Bottom 10%): 0× multiplier

Does not meet minimum expectations

Performance improvement plan required

Ineligible for any bonus payout

Company Performance Matrix:
Financial Metrics (70% weight):

Revenue Growth:

Exceeds Target (>110%): 1.3×

Meets Target (90-110%): 1.0×

Below Target (<90%): 0.6×

Profitability (EBITDA):

Exceeds Target: 1.2×

Meets Target: 1.0×

Below Target: 0.7×

Strategic Objectives (30% weight):

Product/Service Milestones:

All achieved: 1.2×

Most achieved: 1.0×

Few achieved: 0.8×

Customer Satisfaction (NPS):

50: 1.1×

30-50: 1.0×

<30: 0.9×

ANNUAL CYCLE DETAILS
Q1: Goal Setting & Baseline (January - March)
January 15th: Company objectives communicated

February 1st: Individual goal submission deadline

February 15th: Manager approval and calibration

March 1st: Finalized goals in performance system

Q2: Mid-Year Review (April - June)
July 1-15: Formal mid-year checkpoints

Performance Assessment: 50% progress evaluation

Adjustment Window: 15% goal modification allowed

Manager Training: Calibration sessions for consistency

Q3: Year-End Assessment (July - December)
December 1-15: Final performance reviews

Calibration Committees: Cross-department alignment

Preliminary Calculations: Bonus estimates generated

Approval Workflow: Multi-level review process

Q4: Payout Process (January - March)
January: Board approval of company performance

February 1st: Final individual ratings confirmed

February 15th: All calculations completed

March 15th: Bonus payout date

Direct deposit by 5:00 PM local time

Separate pay stub issued

Tax withholding at supplemental rate (22% federal)

SPOT BONUS PROGRAM
Eligibility & Criteria:
Timing: Anytime outside annual cycle

Amount Range: $500 - $5,000

Qualifying Achievements:

Exceptional project completion under tight deadlines

Significant cost savings (>$50,000)

Innovation leading to patent or proprietary advantage

Extraordinary customer service recovery

Cross-functional leadership on critical initiatives

Approval Process:
Nomination: Manager submits detailed justification

Review: HR validates eligibility and compliance

Approval: VP-level authorization required

Processing: 2-week turnaround from approval to payment

Caps & Limitations:
Maximum 2 spot bonuses per employee annually

Cannot exceed 10% of base salary in spot bonuses

Not considered in annual bonus calculations

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS
Pro-Ration Rules:
Partial Year Service: (Months active ÷ 12) × calculated bonus

Promotion During Year: Weighted average of target percentages

Leave of Absence: Service credit suspended during unpaid leave

Guaranteed Minimums:
No guaranteed minimum bonus amount

Company reserves right to modify program annually

Historical payout range: 50-150% of target

Dispute Resolution:
30-day window to raise concerns

Three-member review panel

Final decision within 15 business days

Communication Protocol:
Individual meetings with managers in March

Written confirmation within 5 business days

Online calculator for self-estimation

Dedicated compensation hotline for questions
